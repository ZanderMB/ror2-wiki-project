SURVIVORS

Survivors are the various playable characters available in Risk of Rain 2, each with their own unique abilities. Players start with only the Commando and Huntress playable. The Railgunner becomes immediately playable with ownership of the Survivors of the Void DLC. The Seeker becomes immediately playable with ownership of the Seekers of the Storm DLC. he remaining Survivors are unlocked by accomplishing various Challenges.
